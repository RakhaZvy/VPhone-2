About Us

What is V-Phone?

V-Phone is a company that specializes in smartphones. Our company provides of smartphones suitable for each need that the customers has. The strong features of our products lie in their performance, especially in gaming, camera, and affordable prices while still offering great specifications. Now our products is selling worldwide and we can improve our service because now we making website so that our customers can buy our products from home, and make the transaction easier.

Our Missions and Values

Our missions is to provide, and delivers a products with strong performance especially in gaming, and camera that have affordable prices. Other than that our missions is to make our product, and services more flexible, that's why our company make this website to improves that.

Our company values is ICA (innovation, Customers First, and Affordability):
  1. Innovations: We make an innovation in our products so our products is different than the others phone.
  2. Customers First: Our company always anticipates the needs of our customers, and we continuously listen to and improve based on the feedback and suggestions they provide.
  3. Affordability: Our company is making a products with great specification but we make this product cheaper than the others so that our products is more affordable than the others.
